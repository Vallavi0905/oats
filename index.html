Peppy Tomato
Vegetable Roll
5 10

  • Ingredients
  • Saffola Peppy Tomato Oats - 40g
  • Saffola Active oil - 2tbsp 5ml
  • Cumin seeds - 1tsp 2g
  • Carrot - 1/2 cup 100 g
  • Capiscum & Onion - 1/2 cup 50 g each
  • Spinach Tortilla - 2 x 100 g
  • Method:
  • 1. Follow the directions to cook Saffola Peppy Tomato Oats and set them aside to cool
  • 2. Heat Saffola Active oil in a pan and add cumin seeds,capiscum,onion,salt to taste and black pepper. Cook for 3-4 minutes on a low flame.
  • 3. Keep a spianch tortilla on a plate and spread some oats on top.
